# Users don't read

## Belief

People in a product scan; they don't read. Attention drops fast after the first line, and faster mid-task. Assume one line gets read.

## Evidence

- The whole verdict system (keep/support/defer/cut) exists because of this belief, and it has held up in every walkthrough so far.
- Counter-evidence noted 2026-07-18: session recordings can show users pausing 10+ seconds on error messages. Stressed users at a moment of need may read more than the belief predicts.

## Exceptions

- Moments of need: worried users hunt for "am I okay?" and will read to find it.
- Decision points with money attached.
- Legal and medical content that users arrive intending to read.

## Confidence

High — with a flag: confidence is lower for error states than for marketing and onboarding surfaces. Watch for evidence that splits this belief in two.
