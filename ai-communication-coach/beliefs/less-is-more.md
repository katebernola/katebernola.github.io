# Less is more

## Belief

Cutting a message usually helps more than improving it. The attention freed by a cut goes straight to the messages that remain.

## Exceptions

- Moments where missing information causes guessing: decision points, error recovery. There, the deciding fact must be present — less-but-wrong loses to enough-and-right.
- Reassurance under stress: one fact of comfort ("your other files are safe") earns its space.

## Evidence

- Upload-error case: six messages became three; the survivors got shorter and the standards score rose (19/11/1 → 24/7/0).
- Every review so far has produced more cuts than rewrites.

## Confidence

High — but watch the failure mode: terse can read as cold, and at tense moments cold reads as evasive.
