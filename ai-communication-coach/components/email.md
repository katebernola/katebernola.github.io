# Email

## What it is

The main "later touchpoint" channel — where deferred messages usually land.

## Use it for

- Deferred messages whose trigger arrived.
- Records the user may need again (confirmations, receipts).

## Don't use it for

- Anything the user needs while mid-task in the product. Later channels have weaker attention; what you gain in timing you lose in reach.

## Copy rules

- Subject line carries the whole message — assume the body goes unread.
- One email, one job. If it has two calls to action, it has none.
- Say who it's from in product terms the user recognizes. See [../terminology/product-language.md](../terminology/product-language.md).
